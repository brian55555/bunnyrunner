How to Play

Bunny Runner is an endless runner game where you control a bunny trying to escape a fox!

  • Use SPACE or UP ARROW to jump over obstacles
  • The fox will follow you and try to catch you
  • Reach 10,000 points to win the game
  • You can customize your bunny in the Dressing Room
